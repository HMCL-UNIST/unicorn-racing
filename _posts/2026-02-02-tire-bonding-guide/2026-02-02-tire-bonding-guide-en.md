## Check if the tire is pre-bonded

If the product is labeled as **pre-bonded**, you can use it as-is. If there is no label, bonding may be required.

![Bonded label example](/assets/img/posts/tire-bonding-guide/bonding-label.png)

## How to bond the tire yourself

Follow the video guide below. A jig is helpful but **not required**, so you can still do the process without one.

## Wrap-up

Tire bonding affects both durability and driving stability. Always check the product label first, and if bonding is required, follow the guide carefully to ensure a safe and secure bond.
