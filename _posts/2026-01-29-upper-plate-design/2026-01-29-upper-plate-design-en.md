# Upper Plate Design Files and Details

The upper plate is designed to fit both **Serpent SRX8 GTE '23 1/8 4WD EP** and **Traxxas 740544 Ford Fiesta ST Rally 1/10** chassis.

## Design Overview

- Designed in SolidWorks and shared as SLDPRT and STL files (see shared originals)
- Thicknesses of 5T and 5.5T were chosen to improve sensor data reliability; adjust as needed for your printer
- 3D printing guide: 20% infill, triangle pattern, tested with ABS
- No dedicated BEC mount; we used doubleâ€‘sided silicone tape under the plate
- Plate versions are separated depending on chassis and PC configuration
