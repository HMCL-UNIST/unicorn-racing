Our team achieved **5th place** out of 20 participating teams at the 10th F1TENTH Autonomous Grand Prix, held during ICRA 2022.

**Event:** 10th F1TENTH Autonomous Grand Prix
**Location:** Philadelphia, PA, USA
**Date:** May 23, 2022
**Result:** 5th Place / 20 Teams

This was our first international autonomous racing competition. We competed against teams from universities around the world, demonstrating our autonomous racing algorithms on the F1TENTH platform.

More information: [https://icra2022-race.f1tenth.org/index.html](https://icra2022-race.f1tenth.org/index.html)
